Joseph Albert Nefario (Doctor Nefario)

He is a genius in the universe of Despicable Me. He is the smartest, most powerfull person, even though not everybody knows about him.

We don't know a lot about his past, but we can see that he is very evil and is capable of inventing weapons of mass destruction

He was working with Gru (The villain) throughout the films, helping him by inventing weapons, mixtures, rockets and spacecraft.
