Joseph Albert Nefario (Doctor Nefario)

Dr. Nefario is a character from the animated film series "Despicable Me" and "Minions." He is a mad scientist, genius and inventor who works as Gru's chief technical assistant and gadgeter. Dr. Nefario is known for his quirky and eccentric personality, often coming up with strange inventions to aid Gru in his villainous schemes. Overall, Dr. Nefario is a lovable yet slightly character who adds humor and creativity to the "Despicable Me" and "Minions" franchises.

Facts about Dr. Nefario

  1. He was 90 years old in "Despicable Me 4."
  2. Nefarious means evil, wicked and sinful. It comes from the Latin, where nefas is a combination of ne- "not" and fas, meaning "right" or "divine law."
  3. He was born on August 12th, 1928. That means that we can't be sure that he wasn't participating in the Second World War. We can only wonder which side he was on.
